# Agent Recommendation #8

## Summary
Unsupported causal/temporal inferences stated as fact (4× this week, severity=high)

## Rationale
Add a rule prohibiting the model from asserting conditions it cannot directly verify from the photograph (e.g., adhesive degradation status, recency of damage, granule depletion from a distance). Descriptions must be limited to what is visually confirmable in the image.

## Target
`backend/processor.py:analyze_photos`

## Proposed Diff
```diff
--- a/backend/processor.py
+++ b/backend/processor.py
@@ DESCRIPTION GUIDELINES @@
+
+## RULE — No unsupported causal or temporal inferences
+# Only describe conditions that are directly and unambiguously visible in the
+# photograph. Do NOT add inferences about:
+#   - Recency of damage (e.g., "recent mechanical separation", "recent wind removal")
+#     unless there is an explicit, stated visual indicator (e.g., bright un-oxidized metal).
+#   - Absence of degradation (e.g., "no adhesive degradation visible") — this is a
+#     forensic conclusion the adjuster will make, not the AI.
+#   - Material condition not discernible at the photo's resolution or distance
+#     (e.g., "depleted granules" from a grade-level overview).
+# BAD:  "Wind-lifted tab reveals clean mechanical seal break; no adhesive degradation visible."
+# GOOD: "Wind-lifted tab reveals clean mechanical seal break at bond line."
+# BAD:  "Full removed shingle; weathered surface with depleted granules."
+# GOOD: "Full removed shingle laid in truck bed measured at ~38\" length."

```
