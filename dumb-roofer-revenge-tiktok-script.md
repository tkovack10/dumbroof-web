# "THE DUMB ROOFER REVENGE" — Full TikTok Production Script

**Duration:** 45–60 sec | **Two-act structure** | **Two music cues**
**Purpose:** MVP product showcase ad for dumbroof.ai
**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

---

## ACT I — "THE SETUP" (0:00–0:14)

*Silence or ambient sound only*

### SCENE 1 — THE STEREOTYPE (0:00–0:06)

**Visual Option A (Acted):** Tom or an actor — work boots, beat-up truck, standing outside a house. Phone buzzes. He looks at it. Camera catches his face reading an email.

Cut to phone screen:

> "Claim denied. Insufficient documentation."
> — Allstate Claims Department

His face. He doesn't react. Just stares.

**Visual Option B (Text-only, easier to produce):** Black screen. White text appears word by word:

> The adjuster pulls up. Sees a roofer. Smiles.

Then one more line fades in, smaller, italic:

> *Easy denial.*

**Audio:** Dead silent. Or distant ambient — wind, a truck idling.

### SCENE 2 — THE LAUGH (0:06–0:10)

**Visual:** Quick cuts. Each 1 second.

- An email draft that's clearly unprofessional — typos, no formatting
- A carrier scope: **$20,469.** Stamped FINAL.
- An adjuster on the phone — relaxed, leaning back, not worried
- Text card slams in:

> **They weren't worried. They should have been.**

**Audio:** Still silence. The quiet makes the next moment hit harder.

### SCENE 3 — THE TRANSITION (0:10–0:14)

**Visual:** Close-up on a laptop keyboard. Fingers type out:

> **d-u-m-b-r-o-o-f-.-a-i**

Each letter appears on screen as it's typed. The URL fills the address bar.

🎵 **Quinn XCII — "Kings of Summer"** starts playing on the ENTER key press.

Quick flash: The dumbroof.ai dashboard loads. Clean. Dark UI. Professional.

Then — **3 simple inputs fly in**, each holding 1 second. Big, clean, unmistakable:

| Beat | Input | Visual |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1 | 📐 **MEASUREMENTS** | EagleView report thumbnail dropping into an upload zone |
| 2 | 📸 **PHOTOS** | Grid of damage photos cascading into place |
| 3 | 📋 **CARRIER SCOPE** | Insurance scope PDF sliding in |

Text overlay (holds 1.5 sec):

> **That's it. Every roofer has these.**

Then — finger hits **ENTER**.

Screen flashes white.

**Energy:** This feels like a rocket launching. Quinn XCII is building.

### SCENE 6 — THE QUOTES (0:28–0:34)

**Visual:** Clean cards. Dark background. Credibility moment. Music drops slightly in volume — let these land.

**Card 1 — Meta Global Engineer:**

> "What dumbroof.ai built with AI document intelligence is exactly the kind of applied automation we talk about in theory."
>
> — Senior Engineer, Meta

Small, elegant. Space Mono font. Fades in, holds 2 sec.

**Card 2 — McKinsey & Company:**

> "AI-native platforms that compress expert workflows from weeks to minutes represent the next wave of professional services disruption."
>
> — McKinsey & Company

Same treatment. One beat. Then gone.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:** These quotes must be real or accurately paraphrased from real interactions/published reports. If you have actual beta user quotes from engineers or consultants — use those. **Visual:** Cut back to the roofer from Scene 1.

Same boots. Same truck.

But now he's leaning against it. Arms crossed. Not checking his phone nervously.

*Waiting.*

Phone buzzes.

He looks at it. Camera catches his face again.

This time — the corner of his mouth goes up.

Cut to phone screen:

**Visual:** Black screen.

Below it, Space Mono fades in:

### Editor Notes

- **The silence in Act I is not dead air** — it IS the setup. Do not add music early.
- **Scene 3 is the hero moment** — typing dumbroof.ai + the 3 simple inputs = "this is so easy any roofer can do it"
- **Music sync is everything** — map both tracks in BeatViz before cutting a single frame
- **The contrast between quiet humiliation → Quinn XCII energy → Rick Ross confidence = the entire emotional engine**
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 native. Logo and cards center-frame so they survive any crop.
- **Text:** Inter 900, white, no drop shadows. Let contrast do the work.
