Standard Notes — Asphalt Roof

  • Shingle brand: Owens Corning Duration or GAF Timberline HDZ (approved brands). Color per homeowner selection.
  • Ice & water: Calculated as (eave LF × 3 SF) + (valley LF × 6 SF). Covers 3' at eaves + full valley.
  • Drip edge: Include 5% waste. Cover all eaves + rakes.
  • Steep charge: 7/12–9/12 add STP7 remove + install. 10/12+ consult with Tom.
  • High roof: 2+ stories add HIGH remove + install (per SQ of install area).
  • Ridge vent: RGVC requires Desktop price lookup — mark as supplement if not in original scope.
  • Dumpster: 30-yard via CLN DMPW. Order through Matto.
  • Permits: GEN PRMT — required in most NY/NJ/PA jurisdictions. Matto handles filing.
  • EagleView: Always order EagleView before finalizing quantities. Carrier scope may differ.

Vinyl Siding Replacement — Full Scope

Common brands: CertainTeed, Alside, Mastic. Typical job = 25–60 SQ. Check FYI for color/profile.

Standard Notes — Siding

  • Crew: Norbin runs siding exclusively. Contact him directly. Does not share crews with roofing.
  • Profile: Dutch Lap .044 is standard. Beaded CedarMill if homeowner upgrades. Confirm with FYI.
  • House wrap: Always include Tyvek (SID WRAP) on full replacements. Insurance often approves.
  • Fanfold: Include ½" fanfold foam if going over existing (add R-value, smoother surface).
  • Openings: Subtract window/door area but add J-channel for each opening perimeter.
  • Waste factor: 10% standard for vinyl siding. 15% if lots of cuts (dormers, complex gables).
  • Soffit & fascia: Price from full Xactimate price list — SID SOFV / SFG FASC codes.
  • Supplement items: House wrap, fanfold, corner posts, J-channel often NOT in original carrier scope. Submit supplement.
  • Hardie/fiber cement: Use SID HARR code instead. Greyvin handles specialty installs.

Aluminum Gutter & Downspout Replacement

Standard USARM gutter scope. Crew: Rene (Ice Contractors — handles ALL gutter work).
Standard: 5" K-style aluminum. Colors: White, Brown, Almond. Call Rene for schedule.

Standard Notes — Gutters

  • Crew: Rene (Ice Contractors) handles ALL gutter work. Al contacts him directly. Do not send roofing crew.
  • Standard size: 5" K-style aluminum. Some older homes use 4" — confirm before ordering.
  • Color matching: White is default. Brown or Almond if trim matches. Confirm with homeowner and FYI.
  • Seamless: Ice Contractors fabricates on-site with seamless gutter machine. No seams = fewer leaks.
  • Prices: SFG GUTW (gutter) + SFG DNSW (downspout) are in the USARM price list. Other items check Xactimate Desktop.
  • Gutter guards: Often a supplement item not in original scope. LeafGuard or similar. Submit separately.
  • Fascia damage: If fascia is rotted behind gutters, add SFG FASC R&R. Often supplement-eligible.
  • Spike-to-screw: Standard upgrade. Include if carrier allows — prevents gutters pulling away from fascia.

Cedar Shake Roof Replacement

Specialty work. Crew: Greyvin (independent sub — handles slate, metal & cedar).
Contact Greyvin directly for scheduling. Material lead time: 3–5 days. Price from Xactimate price list.

Standard Notes — Cedar Shake

  • Crew: Greyvin only. Do not send standard asphalt crew — cedar requires specialty knowledge.
  • Material: Western Red Cedar, #1 grade. Confirm 16", 18", or 24" shakes per original spec.
  • Underlayment: 30 lb felt between courses + breathable underlayment at deck. Not synthetic.
  • Ridge: Hand-cut cedar ridge or prefab — check original scope. Insurance typically approves like-for-like.
  • Flashing: All copper or galvanized steel. Never aluminum with cedar (galvanic reaction).
  • Supplement: Cedar is almost always a supplement fight. Document existing material grade. Photos critical.
  • Xactimate codes: RFG WOODA (remove cedar), RFG WOODS (install cedar shake) — check Desktop for current market price.
  • Pricing note: Cedar prices vary widely. Always pull from Xactimate Desktop for current market rate. Not in standard price list.

Slate Roof Replacement

Specialty work. Crew: Greyvin (independent sub — handles slate, metal & cedar).
Highest value work. Lead time: 1–2 weeks for material. Always document existing slate type for insurance match.

Standard Notes — Slate

  • Crew: Greyvin only. Slate work requires certified installer — do not deviate.
  • Slate type: Photograph existing slate. Identify: Vermont (soft), Pennsylvania (hard), imported (Chinese — do NOT match with Chinese slate on insurance claims). Document thickness.
  • Deck: Slate requires solid sheathing (1x4 skip sheating or plywood). Inspect and document deck condition before quoting.
  • Nails: Copper nails only. Never galvanized with slate.
  • Flashing: Copper or lead — document existing material. Insurance must match like-for-like.
  • Supplement: Copper flashing, copper nails, hip & ridge slate, valley metal are all commonly denied and supplemented. Build it all in from the start.
  • Xactimate codes: RFG SLTR (remove slate), RFG SLTI (install slate) — always pull price from Desktop. Varies significantly by market.
  • Partial replacement: If only spot repairs needed — Greyvin can hand-set individual slates. Price per EA not SQ.
