## Known Tactics
- **ITEL pricing instead of Xactimate:** Splits shingle install into separate "Install" (labor) and "Supply" (material) lines at below-market rates. Combined ITEL 3-tab rate of $288.37/SQ is 8% below standard Xactimate $312.92/SQ. This systematically reduces payment.
- **Separate remove + replace instead of R&R:** Lists separate tear-out and replace lines for drip edge, pipe jacks, vents, gutters, downspouts. This can result in lower combined prices vs industry-standard R&R line items.
- **Partial underlayment:** Scoped 9.79 SQ of felt on a 20.66 SQ roof (47% coverage). Code requires full deck.
- **LF-based ice/water instead of SF:** Used LF pricing ($5.24/LF) for a single row at partial eaves instead of SF-based coverage (2 courses at eaves + valleys per code). Covers less than 30% of code-required area.
- **Complete trade exclusion:** Completely excluded the 645 SF corrugated metal roof despite adjuster chalk-marking hail damage on it during inspection. Adjuster inspected and documented damage but excluded it from scope.
- **Partial height allowance:** Applied 2-story charges to only 11.03 SQ of 20.66 SQ (53%). Should apply to full roof area.
- **No ridge/hip cap:** Omitted 128 LF of required ridge and hip cap entirely.
- **No roof-wall flashing:** Omitted all step flashing (46 LF) and counter flashing (30 LF).
- **No O&P:** 0% O&P despite 3 trades.
- **No roofer labor or equipment:** Omitted labor hours and equipment operator.

## Effective Counter-Arguments
1. **ITEL vs Xactimate pricing:** ITEL is a carrier-preferred platform, not industry standard. Contractors use Xactimate pricing. Document the 8% price gap per SQ.
2. **Logical inconsistency — adjuster chalk marks:** Adjuster chalk-marked hail dents on the metal roof during inspection (photo evidence) but excluded the metal roof from scope. The carrier's own inspection proves damage exists.
3. **Under-documentation proof:** Annotated photos showing adjuster stopped marking chalk while additional dents continued beyond. Proves incomplete inspection methodology.
4. **Code compliance (RCNYS):** Underlayment (R905.2.8.2), I&W (R905.2.7.1), flashing (R903.2) — cite specific deficiencies with measured quantities.
5. **Height allowance math:** Simple percentage comparison — carrier covers only 53% of roof at height charges.
6. **O&P entitlement:** 3 trades = GC coordination required.
