## Effective Counter-Arguments

### House Wrap Corner Rule (R703.1/R703.2)
- Carrier approved siding on left elevation only
- RCNYS R703.1/R703.2 requires continuous weather-resistant exterior wall envelope
- Tyvek MUST wrap around outside corners — forced expansion from 1 to 4 elevations
- **Single biggest dollar impact on this claim**

### EPDM Core Sample Testing
- Carrier completely omitted 195 SF flat roof section
- EPDM core sample testing provided irrefutable evidence of storm damage to flat roof membrane
- First claim where core sample testing was used as evidence
- Core samples show membrane degradation patterns consistent with hail impact

### EagleView Measurement Proof
- Carrier's measurements were provably wrong vs EagleView:
  - Fascia: 58 LF (carrier) vs 252 LF (EagleView) — 77% undercount
  - Gutters: 30.75 LF (carrier) vs 211 LF (EagleView) — 85% undercount
- EagleView is an independent third-party measurement — carrier cannot dispute without their own aerial measurement

### 5-Trade O&P Justification
- Flat roofing (EPDM) counts as a separate trade from steep-slope roofing
- 5 distinct trades = O&P clearly justified (10% overhead + 10% profit)
- Carrier's attempt to count only 2 trades is unsupportable

- NYBI26 is the current Xactimate price list for the Binghamton, NY region
