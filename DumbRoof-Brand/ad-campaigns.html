Campaign 1: "The Numbers Don't Lie"

Emotion: Proof. Confidence. Undeniable results.
Bold stat-forward ads. One number, one line, one CTA. Let the results speak. The audience is roofing owners who are tired of promises — show them proof.

Ad Copy Options

  • $20,408 → $943,233. Same claim. Ask Richard.
  • +4,508%. That's not a typo.
  • 15 minutes. 5 documents. $1.167M recovered.
  • $7,500 worth of professionals. $10 worth of AI. Same output.
  • 90% of claims are submitted without evidence. Yours won't be.

Campaign 2: "Did You Ask Richard?"

Emotion: Curiosity. FOMO. Mystery.
Got Milk? style — the tagline IS the ad. People see it and wonder who Richard is. Build brand awareness through intrigue. Richard is the AI that finds what adjusters miss.

Ad Copy Options

  • Did you ask Richard? (that's it — the whole ad)
  • Your adjuster said no. Richard said watch this.
  • Richard doesn't sleep. Richard doesn't forget. Richard doesn't lose.
  • Every carrier has a playbook. Richard read all of them.
  • Ask Richard before they lowball you.

Campaign 3: "The Rep Problem"

Emotion: Frustration → Relief. Recognition.
Scenario-based ads. The audience sees themselves. Every roofing owner knows this pain — most reps can't fight carriers. This campaign says "we get it" and offers the fix.

Ad Copy Options

  • You have 50 reps. 5 understand insurance. Now all 50 do.
  • Your best rep quit last month. His claims knowledge didn't.
  • New hire. Day one. Sends a forensic appeal the carrier actually responds to.
  • Stop training reps to fight carriers. Let Richard do it.
  • They're not bad reps. They just don't have the right tools.

Visual designs coming in Round 2 — neutral backgrounds with Deep Green pain text, Futuristic Pink punchline.

Campaign 4: "Don't Tell Them. Show Them."

Emotion: Authority. Professional credibility.
Product-forward ads. Show what the actual output looks like — forensic reports, code-cited supplements, carrier-specific appeal letters. The documentation IS the ad.

Ad Copy Options

  • Don't tell the adjuster they missed damage. Show them.
  • Code-cited. Photo-annotated. Carrier-specific. In 15 minutes.
  • Your supplement shouldn't look like a spreadsheet. It should look like evidence.
  • This is what $943,233 of carrier movement looks like.
  • Forensic-grade. Not estimate-grade.

Visual designs coming in Round 2 — clean #F9F9F9 backgrounds with document mockups and Deep Green framing.
