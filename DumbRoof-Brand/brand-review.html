Brand Brief

  • Brand: Dumb Roof Technologies LLC / dumbroof.ai
  • Parent of: USA Roof Masters
  • Vibe: Loud, bold, neon — nothing like traditional construction. Billion-dollar brand energy.
  • Logo Style: "DUMB" in white + "ROOF" in neon green — two-tone wordmark
  • Mascot: Richard = lowercase "d" with a face. Simple, geometric, scales to any size.
  • Richard's Personality: Mob boss consigliere meets AI. Powerful, composed, slightly cocky.
  • Campaign: "Did you ask Richard?" / "Ask Richard!" — Got Milk? style
