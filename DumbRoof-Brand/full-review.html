Brand Rules

DO

  • Use loud color with discipline
  • Let product screenshots feel clean and trustworthy
  • Use concise benefit-led headlines
  • Test mascot as support element, not crutch
  • Speak to ROOFERS (the customer)
  • Always show dumbroof.ai prominently

DON'T

  • Look like a traditional roofer logo or truck wrap
  • Overcrowd with too many bright colors equally
  • Lean into cheesy AI robot tropes
  • Use generic SaaS gradients
  • Use Loud Red except for system alerts
  • Redesign Richard's silhouette
