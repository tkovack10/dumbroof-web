# Instagram Grid Strategy — Dumb Roof

## The Rule
Every post goes in position 3, 2, or 1 — right to left. Instagram fills the grid right to left, top to bottom.

**Posting sequence: R → M → L → R → M → L (repeat forever)**

Every 3rd post (3, 6, 9, 12, 15...) is always the off-white neutral in the left column.

## Three Templates

- **Template L** — Off-white `#F9F9F9` bg, black text, minimal style. Tips, how-it-works, process explainers. Quiet and informative.
- **Template M** — Rotating brand colors. Bold statements, data posts, proof ($943,233). High impact.
- **Template R** — Rotating brand colors. Richard moments, "Did you ask Richard?", attitude posts. Brand voice.

## The One Rule
Never post twice in a row. If the sequence breaks, pick up where you left off — don't double-post to fix it. A slightly off grid is fine. A chaotic posting binge to "fix" alignment looks worse.
